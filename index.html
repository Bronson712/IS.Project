About

World traveler. Ski enthusiast. Pickleball enthusiast.

I love to travel! Growing up my family weren't' big travelers. I went on my first plane ride when I was a junior in high school. Since then I have been to eighteen states, the Phillipines, India, South Korea, and I'll be going to Tanzania this summer for a Humanitarian trip.

I love to ski! I've been skiing since I was twelve years old. My family would go to Snowbird every winter, and I fell in love with the sport. Now that I live so close to Sundance it's super convenient to ski there with friends!

In High School I loved to workout, but in college I've lost that motivation. Since coming home from my mission I've started to play pickleball because of my tennis experience in High School.
